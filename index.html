About Me

I've been working in IT at since 2014, currently as a business analyst focusing on custom data integrations and process improvement.

Outside of work, I enjoy recording covers and writing original music. In recent months, I've been exploring web app development as a hobby, building small tools for myself and others to use.

I graduated from DePauw University with both a degree in economics and music, and from IU with a masters of music in voice.
